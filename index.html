How much does it cost?

Auto Comms costs $10 per month, billed monthly. However, sending text messages (SMS) costs an additional $0.05 cents per message block.

How long does the subscription last?

Subscriptions run on a straightforward month-to-month basis. Your account will automatically renew at the start of each cycle.

Can I turn it off at any time?

Yes, you can turn off Auto Comms whenever you want! Just keep in mind that you will still be billed through the end of your current month.

Yes! You have complete flexibility as an admin to run targeted email schedules while keeping the SMS/Text messaging module completely disabled.

Delivery Operations

What is the main difference between Emails and Text Messages here?
Emails let you attach actual files (like your invoice PDFs) and can be styled nicely. Text messages are simple and quick, but they are limited by mobile phone companies to basic text only.
How exactly am I charged for Text Messages?
Sending texts is a premium feature. Every standard text message is 160 characters long. If you send a super long text, the phone company splits it into multiple messages, and you are charged 5 cents ($0.05) for each part.
Can I run more than one schedule at the same time?
Absolutely! As an admin, you can set up completely different schedules targeting different groups of people, and they will all run safely at the exact same time.
How do the audience filters work?
When you are making a schedule, you get to choose exactly who gets the message. For example, you can tell the system to skip anyone who already uses Auto-Pay, or skip people whose credit cards are expired.
Does the actual invoice file get sent in the text message?
No, mobile phone systems can't handle large files well. Instead, your text message will include a secure web link. When your customer taps the link on their phone, it opens their invoice safely.
What do the 'Pending', 'Delivered', and 'Failed' numbers mean?
'Delivered' means the message successfully reached your customer. 'Failed' means something went wrong (like a bad email address or a fake phone number). 'Pending' means the message is just waiting in line to be sent out soon.
